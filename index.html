What is Kundalini Activation?

IKA is an ancient healing modality, a powerful form of energy work with the potential to induce transcendental states of awareness and facilitate deep personal transformation.

It is an activation of your Life Force energy. It opens the path to profound awareness, harmony, and wholeness.

How Does It Work?

I hold individual and group sessions both online and offline, during which you immerse yourself in a state of deep relaxation.
The energy and consciousness of higher dimensions, transmitted during the session, begin to align and awaken your own Kundalini energy, activating your dormant potential and initiating your inner self-healing mechanism.

During the activation process, energy blockages are removed, and the life force within you is awakened.

No prior experienceis required.

All you need is an openness to embrace a new experience.

What happens during the session?

*

Before the session, it's important to complete some preparation. You can find it in the event description when you sign up.

1

During the Kundalini activation session, you lie on a mat, relax your body and mind, and let go of all expectations.

2

Your eyes are closed, your muscles are relaxed, and you remain open to any sensations, feelings, and energy manifestations.

3

The energy transmission lasts for an hour, accompanied by music throughout the session.

4

During a group session, I work simultaneously with the group as a whole and with each participant individually.

5

At times, I may touch energy centers or meridian points on your body. If you are uncomfortable with physical touch, I can work with you at a distance.

6

You will remain conscious throughout the session, although your mind may occasionally shift into a meditative state.

7

After the session, there is a sharing circle where everyone can exchange experiences, share insights, and ask questions.

The full session duration is 2 hours (including an introduction, one-hour energy transmission, and a sharing circle).
